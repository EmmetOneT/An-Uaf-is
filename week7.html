DevLog

Week 1

Added the close and open fade in/ out from my first puzzle. When the puzzle is complete a small cinematic of the player blinking and looking towards the island plays. Disabling the players controls and mouse cursor allowed for this. When the animation is finished, the player loads into the next scene. Started outside mansion scene. Acquired necessary assets. Built terrain, water and mansion exterior. Completed building of outside area. Added fog. Went back to Boat scene to make text more legible by adding TextMeshPro. Added NPC with dialogue. Brought the boat in and reanimated it. Generated lighting for the scene. Created a new scene to try to replicate the Resident evil style door open transitions. Added animation to doors and camera. Created sound in audacity of door creaking open. Added scene load from the doors of the mansion to the scene of the doors opening to the main hallway of the mansion. Generated lighting. Set up a player “Press e to interact message”. Created the Mansion main hall. Added and generated lighting. Added skybox. Added ceiling, walls and floors for first and second floor of mansion. Openable doors and props for sitting room and started on study/library.
