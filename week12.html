DevLog

Week 1

Created more squared off fog for the outdoor levels to more recreate the old PS1 style graphics. Finished Mansion grounds scene. Continued creating the new materials for the jittering shader. Finished mansion and almost all the attic area, Finished materials for the dithering and jittering. Optimised the resolution settings in the main menu. Fixed lighting on the boat scene. Fixed lighting on mansion grounds scene by adding second directional light. Found a way to get out of bounds so had to fix this. Bug fixes & tidying up. Had to fix the player teleport to attic. Changed teleport from tween position to move position to a cube. Had to add the final enemies death which proved to be a challenge. Added 3d sound to zombies which stops on death. Fixed and baked lighting. Fixed some dialog. set puzzle to 3x3. Added an outline to visible objects that the player can pickup. Turn outline off for items such as the flashlight and pistol when the player equips them.
